About

He loves mathematics and understands it deeply. This website and the YouTube channel are designed to work together. Their purpose is to help people understand mathematics more clearly. The lessons focus on foundations rather than the question formats of particular exams. The learning materials are based on openly licensed books published by universities and open education organizations. Except for content reserved for YouTube channel members, access is completely free.
